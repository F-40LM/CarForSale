1992 Ferrari F40 LM For Sale

This is a once in a lifetime opportunity to purchase an extremely rare 1992 Ferrari F40 LM! This example has been maintained by a team of experts from Maranello, Italy and has seen racing in the 1995 - 1997 IMSA Racing series, including a 2nd place result in the 1996 champtionship.

After a complete restoration in 1998, this car has seen limited track day usage and has undergone all Ferrari recommended maintenance. In 2008 a Wall St trader crashed the car during a queilude induced fit of rage after significant stock market losses. The car was fully repaired per manufacturer specifications however due to the incident and history as a race car, the car will be sold with a rebuilt title.
